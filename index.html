Welcome to my Towers of Hanoi game!

To play, open the console, and use "moveDisc(#, #);"

There are 3 pegs printed in the console

The goal is to get all the discs, 5 4 3 2 1, onto a different peg

Using moveDisc(1, 2) will move the disc from the first peg onto the second

Try it out and see if you can win the game
