Welcome to my Towers of Hanoi game

Instructions:

  • To play, refer to the 3 cards below, use 'Move Disk' to move disks around the pegs represented by the cards.

  • The goal is to get all the disks on the first peg onto any other peg. You cannot place a larger disk onto a smaller one.

  • Use the dropdown menus to make your selections, try it out, and see if you can win the game!
